About


The Workshop of AI and Public Policy will be held in July 22 2017, TsingHua University, Beijing, China. This Workshop aims to discuss the trending topics about artificial intelligence and public policy, with special focus on science policy. In the morning of 22 July 2017, we have four guest speakers to give their keynote speeches; and in the afternoon, there will be two parallel sessions. This workshop is collaborately organized by the Swarma Club, Knowledge Lab, Department of Sociology, University of Chicago, and the School of Public Policy and Management, Tsinghua University.
